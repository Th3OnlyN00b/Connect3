Connect 3

Rules

  • Players take turns inserting chips into a 5 by 4 board.
  • If one of the players connects three clips horizontally, vertically, or diagonially they win.
  • If the board gets filled and no one has connected three, the game ends with a tie.

AI Types

  • Human: Play against another human
  • Random: Play against an AI that will place chips randomly
  • Auto-Win: Play against an AI that will always win
  • Minimax: Play against an AI that will try to avoid lossing at all cost
  • Pruned Minimax: Play against an AI that will try to avoid lossing at all but faster

Hardware

For this project, there is a physical board that you put tokens into, the board will detect where the tokens are and record it (if you are playing against a human) and map it onto the website. If you are playing against an AI, the hardware is not used.
